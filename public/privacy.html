Privacy Policy

Introduction

Josh Desk is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Slack workspace ID and basic workspace information
  • User IDs of workspace members who use Josh Desk
  • Office attendance schedules submitted through the app
  • Workspace settings such as office location and timezone
  • Basic usage data to improve the service

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Josh Desk service
  • To display office attendance information to team members
  • To send notifications and reminders about scheduling
  • To update Slack statuses based on your schedule
  • To show relevant weather information for office days

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in a SQLite database. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with our legal obligations.

Data Deletion

When you uninstall Josh Desk from your workspace, we automatically delete all associated data including schedules, settings, and user information.

Third-Party Services

Josh Desk uses the following third-party services:

  • Slack API for core functionality
  • Weather API for weather forecasts
  • Google Places API for office location geocoding

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
